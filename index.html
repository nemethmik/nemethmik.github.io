PRIVACY POLICY FOR TIVA11 MOBILE APPLICATIONS

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Man Over Board (“Application”) for mobile devices that was created by Tiva11 Mobile Solutions.  The Application is  a wearable application and when started it automatically records the GPS location and starts tracking where Man Over Board (MOB) happened by showing the direction (bearing) and distance in meters. Even when the application is not active, it remembers the last MOB location for 30 minutes, and when resumed it continues tracking. It has two screens, the first one showing the details of the MOB event: MOB location, current location, current time (since this is a clock), the expired seconds since the MOB event, the distance and the bearing. The other screen shows a compass. You can simply swipe from left to right and back between the screens. The application is not sending the MOB location to anywhere. 

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information 

The Application obtains geolocation information automatically from the device when you download, install and startthe Application. There is no registration with us at all.

Since we have no registration, when using the Application, you don't provide  your name, email address, age, user name, password and other information; we are not collecting credit card information at all.

Automatically Collected Information 

The application collects GPS location while it is running and shows the MOB location and your current location. It saves the MOB location on the wearable device for 30 minutes 

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

When you visit the mobile application, we use GPS technology (or other similar technology) to determine your current location in order to determine the distance and bearing from your current location and the MOB location. But, we are not identifying any additional information, your city you are located within and we are not displaying a location map with any advertisements. We will not share your current location with other users or partners. In a future release, you may have the option to send the MOB location via SMS to another person's phone, but it's absolutely under your control. The Application doesn't initiate any communications outside the wearable device.

If you do not want us to use your location for the purposes set forth above, you should can simply revoke the Geolocation authorization from the Application and uninstall it.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

No. We will not collect any data from the device, and it will not be shared to any other parties.

What are my opt-out rights?

You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling the Application. You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace or network.

Data Retention Policy, Managing Your Information
